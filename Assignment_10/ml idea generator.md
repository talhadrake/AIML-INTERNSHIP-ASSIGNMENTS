# Description : Suggest ML problems in college, healthcare, shopping and describe input → output.

1️⃣ College – Student Performance Prediction
# Problem Idea:
     Predict whether a student will pass or fail.

 Input: Attendance, internal marks, assignment scores, study hours
 Output: Pass / Fail (or predicted final marks)

# Why useful?
    Teachers can identify weak students early and give extra support.

2️⃣ Healthcare – Disease Prediction
# Problem Idea:
    Predict if a person may have diabetes.

 Input: Age, weight, blood sugar level, blood pressure
 Output: High risk / Low risk of diabetes

# Why useful?
    Helps in early detection and prevention.

3️⃣ Shopping – Product Recommendation
# Problem Idea:
    Recommend products to customers.

Input: Customer’s past purchases, search history
Output: Suggested products (like “You may also like…”)

# Why useful?
    Improves customer experience and increases sales.
